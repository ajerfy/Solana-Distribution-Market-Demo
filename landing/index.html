The Problem

Binary markets discard your knowledge.

Ask "Will the Lakers–Celtics game go over 220?" and you're forced into yes or no. But you know far more than that.

Standard sportsbook

Forced binary, buried vig

You think the total will land around 224. But the line is fixed at 220.5 and baked with a 5% house edge. You can't express where you think it peaks or how certain you are.

The Math

Why the odds are always better here.

This isn't marketing — it follows from information theory. Distribution markets are strictly superior to binary markets for anyone with real forecasting skill.

5–8%

The vig tax in every binary bet

Standard sportsbooks embed 5–8% vig into every line. To break even you must be right 52–54% of the time before you capture a single dollar of your real edge. Parabola charges only for KL-divergence from the crowd — if you're well-calibrated, you pay almost nothing.

1 bit

How much of your knowledge a binary captures

Over/under is a single bit: above or below a threshold. Every dimension of your knowledge — where the peak is, how certain you are, the tail behavior — is thrown away. A distribution bet uses the full continuous density. Under a logarithmic scoring rule, expected profit scales as KL(you ‖ crowd), which integrates your advantage across all moments.

σ²

The calibration premium

If your σ is half the crowd's, you're more certain — a real informational edge. Binary markets give you nothing for that certainty. Here, tighter σ means lower collateral required, which means higher return on capital for the same directional bet. Proper scoring rules (Brier, logarithmic) are uniquely incentive-compatible: the optimal strategy is to report your true belief.

How It Works

Four steps. Infinite nuance.

01

Pick a market

Sports totals, macro data releases, crypto prices, election margins — any outcome with a numeric realization.

02

Submit a distribution

Choose μ (where you expect the outcome) and σ (how certain you are). More conviction → tighter σ → less collateral.

03

Sign on Solana

Your distribution is an on-chain transaction. The AMM quotes you immediately against the current crowd distribution.

04

Settle and collect

A proper scoring rule pays proportional to how close your distribution was to the realized outcome. Precision compounds.

Perpetual Estimation Markets

Trade the distribution continuously.

Distribution perps let you hold a continuous long or short on how the crowd's collective distribution evolves — a perpetual on crowd wisdom itself.

AMM distribution vs Pyth oracle anchor
Long +0.014%/hr
Short −0.014%/hr

The AMM holds a distribution anchored to a live Pyth oracle. When the collective market μ drifts away from the oracle anchor, a funding rate proportional to KL-divergence is charged — pulling it back, continuously.

  • Long: profits when the crowd distribution shifts above the oracle anchor. Collect funding when markets are unduly bearish.
  • Short: profits when the crowd distribution shifts below anchor. Earn when consensus is too optimistic.
  • KL-divergence funding: the rate scales with the full distributional distance from the oracle — a principled, manipulation-resistant signal impossible in binary perps.
  • No expiry: hold indefinitely. Exit any time by taking the opposing side.
