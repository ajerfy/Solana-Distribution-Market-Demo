How It Works

Four steps, infinite nuance.

01

Pick a market

Browse open estimation markets: sports totals, macro data releases, crypto prices, election margins.

02

Submit a distribution

Choose your mean (what you expect) and sigma (how certain you are). More conviction → tighter sigma → lower collateral required.

03

Sign on Solana

Your distribution is submitted as an on-chain transaction. No intermediary. The AMM quotes you immediately.

04

Settle and collect

When the outcome resolves, the proper scoring rule pays out based on proximity. The closer your distribution, the bigger your reward.

Perpetual Estimation Markets

Trade the distribution continuously.

Distribution perps let you hold a long or short position on how a market's collective distribution evolves — like a perp on crowd wisdom itself.

AMM curve vs Anchor (Pyth oracle)
Long +0.014%/hr
Short −0.014%/hr

In a Parabola perp, the AMM holds a distribution anchored to a live oracle (e.g. Pyth BTC/USD). When the market's collective μ drifts away from the anchor, a funding rate is charged to bring it back — just like a traditional perp funding rate, but over a full distribution.

  • Long: profits when the crowd distribution shifts higher than the oracle anchor. Funding is paid to you when the crowd is bearish.
  • Short: profits when the crowd distribution shifts lower. You collect funding when the crowd is overly optimistic.
  • KL-divergence funding: the rate is proportional to the KL-divergence between the AMM distribution and the oracle anchor — a principled, manipulation-resistant signal.
  • No expiry: hold your position as long as you want. Exit any time by taking the other side.
